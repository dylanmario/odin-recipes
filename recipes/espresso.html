Espresso

This one hack the day doesn't want you to know about!

A classic. Once you've tried this espresso you won't be able to start your day without one.

Smooth, balanced and just the right amount of 'get going' in a cup you can shot down as soon as you get out of bed!

Ingredients

  • Ground beans

Directions

  1. Take ground beans
  2. Tamp
  3. Extract for 26-28 seconds
  4. Enjoy, the day is now yours

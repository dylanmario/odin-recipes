Lasagne

Worlds best lasagne

You won't be able to stop at one piece. This lasagne is unlike any you've tried before, and may not try again!

Perfected over time, this lasagne will even win over your worst enemies.

Ingredients

  • Mince
  • Secret sauce
  • Pasta sheets
  • Grated cheese

Directions

  1. Brown mince in saucepan
  2. Add secret sauce
  3. Reduce saucepan to low heat
  4. Leave to simmer for 8 hours
  5. When the mince is ready to take off, prepare a baking dish
  6. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees
  7. Remove mince from the stove and add a layer to the baking dish
  8. Add a layer of pasta sheets
  9. Add another layer of mince, then pasta sheets
  10. Repeat these steps until all the mince is used
  11. Finish with a layer of grated cheese
  12. Place in oven to bake until the cheese on top is golden brown
  13. Serve and enjoy!

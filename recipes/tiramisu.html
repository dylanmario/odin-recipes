Tiramisu

She will tiramisu you no more!

Sure to win over whoever you share this delicious tiramisu with, it's truly as delicous as it looks!

An old family recipe that I am about to make up. It will leave your stomach full, and your heart fuller.

Ingredients

  • 6x egg yolks
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup cream
  • 1 cup mascarpone
  • As many ladyfingers as you can fit in the bowl
  • 1/2 cup coffee flavoured liqueur
  • 6 shots of espresso
  • As much cocoa powder as your heart desires

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Whip mascarpone with half of the coffee liqueur
  3. Add a layer of mascarpone to the bottom of a bowl
  4. Layer ladyfingers on top
  5. Pour espresso over the top
  6. Fill in gaps and cover the top with mascarpone
  7. Sprinkle with cocoa powder
  8. Enjoy!
